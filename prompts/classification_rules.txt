When reading an email:
- If it contains resume attachments, skills, or job applications -> New Candidate
- If it references previous emails or checking status -> Follow-up
- If it mentions interview, time slots, or meeting links -> Interview response
- If it is promotional or irrelevant -> Spam

Urgent emails:
- Mention deadlines
- Mention interviews today or tomorrow
- Mention immediate availability
- Mention withdrawal of application
- Priority or high-importance flags

Professional Response Guidelines:
- Be polite and concise.
- Acknowledge receipt.
- Do not promise anything specific without internal review.
- Suggest next steps if appropriate (e.g., "we will review and get back to you").
